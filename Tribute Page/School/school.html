Okmalumkoolkat's Stage Names


Okmalumkoolkat, whose real name is Smiso Brian Mkhasibe, is a South African rapper known for his unique style
and contributions to the music scene. He has several stage names that reflect his artistic persona,
including Future Mfana, Smart Mampara, Mlazi Milano, and Don Spovington.
These names are often used interchangeably with Okmalumkoolkat, showcasing his versatility as an artist.
