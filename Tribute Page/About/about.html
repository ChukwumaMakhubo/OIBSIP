About Okmalumoolkat


Okmalumkoolkat, born Smiso Zwane in Umlazi, Durban, South Africa,
is a versatile artist known for his innovative blend of electronic beats with traditional South African influences.
Rising to fame as a member of Dirty Paraffin, he gained widespread acclaim with his solo career, marked by hits like "Gqi" and "Amalobolo."
Despite facing legal challenges, Okmalumkoolkat remains a significant figure in South African music and culture, celebrated for his unique style and creative vision.
