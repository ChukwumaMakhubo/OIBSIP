Okmalumoolkat's Music


Okmalumkoolkat's music blends electronic beats with traditional South African sounds like kwaito and gqom.
His breakthrough mixtape "Holy Oxygen" includes hits like "Gqi" and "Amalobolo." His debut studio album "Spiritual Food"
and collaborative album "Mlazi Milano" further showcase his unique style.
He also collaborates on singles, bringing together different musical styles.
Known for energetic live performances, Okmalumkoolkat continues to captivate audiences with his innovative approach
to music production and lyrical storytelling.



By Chukwuma
